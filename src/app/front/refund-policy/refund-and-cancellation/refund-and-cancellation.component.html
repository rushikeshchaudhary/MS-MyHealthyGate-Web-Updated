Refund & Cancellation Policy

MHG cancellation policy makes booking, cancelling, and rescheduling easy. MHG will honor a full refund for any of our visits when cancelled within the following time constraints that the provider accepts. Each provider will assign their own refund policy and time frame accepted for cancellations.

As a general rule, for online, face to face and home visits you should notify cancellation or rescheduling of an appointment at least 24 hours prior to the schedules visit, otherwise as fees are applicable.Once our MHG admin confirms with the provider that the visit did not take place, refund will be processed and may take up to ten working days.

You may cancel your subscription to Membership Services at any time up to forty-eight (72) hours before the applicable renewal processing date of your subscription, by emailing admin@myhealthygate.com directly requesting a cancellation. If you cancel the Membership Services and your subscription term has not expired, you may continue to use the Membership Services until the end of your then-current subscription monthly or annual term and your subscription will not be renewed after your then-current term expires. However, you won't be eligible for a prorated refund of any portion of the recurring subscription fee paid for the then-current recurring subscription term.

We are unable to offer refunds for the following:

  • No-shows or missed appointments
  • Refund requests due to not receiving a prescription or disagreement with the clinical outcome of the visit
  • Surgical procedures with specific scheduling requirements
  • Any request made 10 days after the original purchase date

If you make a same-day appointment by mistake and need to cancel, MHG will refund you if you request a cancellation within one hour of completing the purchase.
